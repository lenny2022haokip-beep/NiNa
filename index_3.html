Our Story

Threads of Identity

NiNa was born from a deep reverence for Manipuri textile heritage — a tradition stretching back generations, encoded in every diamond, X-motif, and geometric repeat of the weavers of Manipur.

Our three signature patterns — Saipikhup, Thangnang, and Mongvom — are not mere decoration. Each motif carries cultural meaning, a language spoken in thread, passed down from artisan to artisan.

We bring these living patterns into modern accessories: bandanas, scarves, bags, and pins — designed to be worn daily, carrying a piece of heritage into the world.
